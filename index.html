Spring / Deep Cleaning

Thorough top-to-bottom clean. Perfect for seasonal cleaning or special occasions.

View Details
Bedrooms & Living
  • Remove dust from ceilings and walls
  • Wipe down skirting boards
  • Clean doors, door frames, and handles
  • Clean all built-in wardrobes: shelves, doors, tracks
  • Clean all blinds (venetian, roller, vertical, timber)
  • Clean windows, window frames, and tracks (brush + vacuum + wipe)
  • Clean both sides of all window glass (outside where accessible)
  • Dust curtains and rails
  • Clean power outlets and light switch panels
  • Wipe down all light fittings and coverings
  • Clean air-con external surfaces and washable filters (where possible)
  • Full vacuum of all floors, including corners and under furniture
  • Mop hard floors (tiles / timber / vinyl)
Kitchen General
  • Remove grease, dirt, fingerprints from all surfaces
  • Clean and sanitise benchtops
  • Clean inside and outside of all cupboards and drawers
  • Clean sink: remove water stains, limescale, polish to shine
  • Polish taps and fixtures
  • Clean splashback (tile or glass), removing oil and grime
Cooktop (Gas / Electric / Ceramic)
  • Clean drip trays and grates (gas)
  • Wipe and polish cooktop surface
  • Clean control knobs and surrounding areas
Rangehood
  • Degrease external hood surface
  • Clean light and switches
Bathroom
  • Remove soap scum and limescale from shower screens
  • Clean wall tiles, remove mould in grout
  • Clean sink and vanity
  • Clean and streak-free shine on mirrors
  • Clean inside and outside of cabinets
  • Polish taps and metal fittings
  • Clean drain areas
  • Deep clean toilet: bowl, seat, hinges, base
  • Dust and wipe exhaust fan (external)
Laundry
  • Clean laundry tub and tap fixtures
  • Wipe walls and splashback areas
  • Clean washing machine space and surrounding dust
  • Wipe exhaust vents and surrounding areas
Additional Options
  • Carpet steam cleaning
  • Wall mark removal (light to medium)
  • Oven cleaning
  • Garage cleaning
  • Balcony / outdoor area (wipe handrails, mop tiled balcony areas)
  • Wipe remaining furniture (if any)
  • Wiping down the inside of the refrigerator
